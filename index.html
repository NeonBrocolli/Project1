♦ Instructions: ♦

To begin the player must make a bet using their pool of credits. Below are choices of bets between five, ten and all in.

After making a bet the player can spin the reels using either the spin button or the lever for a chance to win more credits.

The reels will then show randomized images and in order to win, the reels must have matching images.

The player will recieve partial wins if only two match on one side or the other. If the player doesn't get a match and have no more credits the game ends :(. Test your luck!
